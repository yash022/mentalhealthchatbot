Stress Detection Pattern Analysis

  • Increased usage of negative emotional words
  • Shorter, more abrupt responses
  • Decreased cognitive complexity in language
  • Focus shifting to immediate concerns

Anxiety Detection Pattern Analysis

  • Increased usage of uncertainty words
  • Excessive self-referential language
  • Future-focused temporal orientation
  • Repeated checking or reassurance seeking

Weekly Stress Pattern Trends

Our data shows stress typically peaks mid-week and before deadlines, with decreases during weekends.
